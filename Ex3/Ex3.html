How can I cook spaghetti?

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
    • -use at least 4 quarts of water for every pound of noodles.
    • -put on high heat.
  2. Salt the water with at least a tablespoon - more is fine.
  3. Add pasta.
  4. Stri the pasta.
  5. Drain the pasta.
